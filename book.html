Straight answers

Questions we get a lot.

Do you work on steep lots?

Steep is normal out here. Western North Carolina doesn't hand out flat ground for free, and that's most of what we grade — tight access, hard slopes, tricky lines. If a lot has a driveway to it, we can probably work it.

How much does grading cost?

It depends on the ground — slope, access, rock, how much dirt actually has to move. We won't guess at a number over the phone, because a guess isn't worth much to you. The on-site walk-through is free: Jon looks at your property and gives you a straight price before anything starts.

How soon can you start?

Depends on the job and what's already on the schedule. Ask when you request your estimate — Jon will give you a real date, not a runaround.

What areas do you serve?

Canton, Waynesville, Clyde, Candler, Asheville, Leicester, Maggie Valley, Sylva — Haywood and Buncombe counties and the lots in between. Not sure if you're in range? Ask. Chances are we've already worked near you.

Send the estimate form and you're talking to Jon — not a call center. A direct email line is on the way.
